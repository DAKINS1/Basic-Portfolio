About Me

I am a professional freelance artist, based in Huntington Beach, CA. I create realistic portraitures with a ball-point pen, which are then "dressed" in acrylic paint, hand-painted in a block print pattern. I am taking my passion and skills in Illustrative Art and my love and excitement for Technology into a new career direction in Web Pogramming and Design.


"When you take technology and mix it with art, you always come up with something innovative." -Robert Rodriguez
